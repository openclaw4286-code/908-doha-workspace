Design System · v0.9

908 Doha

A Gulf-modern design system for calm, premium fintech. Benchmarked from Toss TDS Mobile, rebuilt on 908 Blue #0064FF, neutral ink-on-paper, and a signature ease-soft curve that makes every state change feel like breath.

Principles

Quiet by default, precise when it counts. The system uses color sparingly — one blue, four semantic states, and a neutral ramp from pure white to pure black — so motion carries the personality.

Every state change earns a curve. Nothing teleports. Press gives physical feedback. Numbers interpolate. Sheets settle.

Slots over configuration. List/Board rows expose left, contents, right — composition instead of 30 props.
